## ___Wireless Home Router___
- Serves as an
	- __Access point__ - 802.11a/b/g/n/ac wireless access
	- __Switch__ - Four-port, full-duplex, __10/100/1000__ Ethernet switch
	- __Router__ - Default gateway
- Sends __SSID (Shared Service Set Identifier)__ - Devices who discover SSID attempt to associate and authenticate with it to access the local network and internet.
