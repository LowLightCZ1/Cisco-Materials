## ___Types of Wireless Networks___
- __WPAN (Wireless-Personal-Area-Network)__
	- Low powered transmitters 
	- Short-range network -> 20 to 30 ft. (6 to 9 meters)
	- __Bluetooth an ZigBee__ - Commonly used
	- 802.15 standard
	- 2.4-GHz frequency
- __WLAN (Wireless LANs)__
	- Medium-sized network -> 300 ft.
	- Used at __home, offices__.
	- 802.11 standard
	- 2.4 or 5-GHz frequency
- __WMAN (Wireless MANs)__
	- large-size network 
	- Good for providing wireless access to a metropolitan city. 
	- Specific licensed frequencies.
- __WWAN (Wireless-Wide-Area-Network)__
	- Extensive geographic area.
	- national and global communication.
	- Specific licensed frequencies.

##  ___Wireless Technologies___
- Uses the unlicensed radio spectrum to __send or receive__ data.
- Accessible to anyone who has any wireless technology in the device they're using
- __Bluetooth__
	- 802.15 WPAN standard
	- Uses __device-pairing__ process
	- Communication distance -> 300 ft. (100m)
	- ___BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy)___ - Supports multiple network technologies 
	- ___Bluetooth Basic/Enhanced Rate (B/EDR)___ - Supports point-to-point topologies, for audio streaming

## ___802.11 Standard___
- Define how radio frequencies are used for wireless links.
- __one antenna__ - 2.4, 5 GHz
- __multiple antennas__ - higher frequencies, require __APs (Access points)__
- MIMO (Multiple-input and multiple-output)
